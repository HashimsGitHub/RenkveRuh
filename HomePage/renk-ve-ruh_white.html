⚜️   RENK VE RUH by UZMA Khan   ⚜️

Renk ve Ruh — Color and Soul — is a living archive of Turkey's handmade home-craft traditions. Each piece in our gallery is the work of a named artisan, rooted in a specific village, city, or mountain community, carrying forward a lineage of skill that stretches back centuries.

From the cobalt kilns of İznik to the silent looms of the Taurus highlands; from the marbling trays of Istanbul to the coppersmith lanes of Gaziantep — we seek the makers whose hands hold a culture's memory, and bring their work into homes that honour it.
